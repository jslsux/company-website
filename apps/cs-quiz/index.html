Support and contact information for the iOS revision app.

About the app

GCSE & A Level CS Quiz helps UK students revise Computer Science with exam-style quizzes, flashcards, key revision points, and step-by-step algorithm visualisers across AQA, OCR, Edexcel, Eduqas, and WJEC.

One module is free for each qualification. A single lifetime purchase unlocks full access to every module for both GCSE and A Level.

Not affiliated with any exam board.
