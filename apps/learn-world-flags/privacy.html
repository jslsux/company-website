Privacy Policy

Learn World Flags

Last updated: 29 June 2026

Scott Enterprises Ltd ("we", "us", "our") operates the Learn World Flags iOS app ("the App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

Summary

  • No account is required.
  • Learning progress, streaks, and settings are stored on your device.
  • If you subscribe to Pro, Apple processes payment and RevenueCat validates your entitlement.
  • We do not sell your data or use it for cross-app advertising tracking.
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs in this app.

Information stored on your device

The App stores the following locally on your iPhone or iPad using on-device storage only:

  • Spaced-repetition progress for each country and flag
  • Daily challenge completion and streak data
  • Onboarding and app preference flags
  • Quiz and learn session history needed for progress tracking

This information is not transmitted to our servers because we do not operate user accounts or a backend database for learning progress.

Purchases

If you subscribe to Pro, payment is processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not receive your payment card number or bank details.

We use RevenueCat to validate subscriptions and unlock Pro features. RevenueCat may receive purchase history and an anonymous app user identifier associated with your Apple ID purchase record for app functionality and purchase analytics. We do not receive your name or email address through the App.

What we do not collect

  • Name, email address, phone number, or postal address through the App
  • Precise or coarse location
  • Contacts, photos, health data, browsing history, or search history
  • Advertising identifiers for cross-app tracking

Children

The App is suitable for learners of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App. Because no account is required and progress stays on-device, parents and guardians can delete app data by removing the App from the device.

Data retention and deletion

On-device progress and settings remain on your device until you delete the App or reset app data. Purchase and subscription records are retained by Apple and RevenueCat according to their policies.

Third-party services

  • Apple App Store / StoreKit — payment processing and purchase restoration
  • RevenueCat — subscription validation and entitlement management

Tracking

We do not use the App to track you across other companies' apps or websites for advertising purposes.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the date above.
