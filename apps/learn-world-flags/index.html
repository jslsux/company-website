Learn World Flags

Support and contact information for the iOS flag-learning app.

About the app

Learn World Flags helps you master the flags, countries, and capitals of the world through spaced-repetition learning, daily challenges, and flexible practise modes including flashcards and multiple-choice quizzes.

Europe is free to learn and practise. A Pro subscription unlocks every continent, full progress tracking, and premium learn sessions across all countries.

Available in English, German, Spanish, French, Dutch, and Italian.

Purchases & restore

  • Open the app → Settings → Restore purchases if Pro access is missing after reinstalling.
  • Pro access is tied to your Apple ID and managed through the App Store.
  • For billing or cancellation, review subscriptions in your Apple ID settings.
